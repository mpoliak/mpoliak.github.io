I am an incoming PhD student at MIT Brain and Cognitive Sciences. I study psycholinguistics, or what processes in the mind are involed in language production and comprehension. Specifically, I study language as a social act and how our knowledge of our conversation partners affects how we comprehend and produce language. This will get more concrete over my time in graduate school :)

In the past, I have worked with Anthony Yacovone and Dr. Jesse Snedeker on language development and applying neural decoding to analysis of ERP data. I have also worked with Dr. Mahzarin Banaji for several years on implicit gender-fame stereotyping and implicit attitude acquisition.
